# Love Alaoui

Love Alaoui is a romantic digital ritual brand for couples.

## The Better Conversations Deck

The Better Conversations Deck is a one-time digital purchase with 120 guided prompts for date nights, weekly check-ins, and everyday moments when couples want something deeper than small talk.

## Brand World

Mochi and Mallow guide the emotional tone of the experience.

- Mochi brings softness, empathy, and calmer check-ins.
- Mallow brings warmth, momentum, and playful energy.
- Together they create a cutesy, premium ritual world for couples.

## Main Routes

- `/` landing page
- `/signup` create account
- `/login` log in
- `/checkout/success` purchase success
- `/my-deck` ritual home
- `/about` brand story
- `/privacy` privacy policy
- `/contact` support contact

## Design Notes

- Premium, cutesy, soft, romantic
- No real-life photography in the core website direction
- Character-led and color-led presentation
- Primary pink accent: `#FFC0C2`
- Company brown accent: `#AC7C61`
